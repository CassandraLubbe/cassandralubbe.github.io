What There is to Know About Me

Freelance Ghost Writer

As a ghost writer, I do all the heavy lifting while my clients sit back and relax (occasionally, checking in to see whether we're still on the same page). When I say heavy-lifting, I really mean heavy-lifting. It's my job as a ghost writer to develop a high-quality outline, research the topic with intensity and meaning to ensure it's unique and relevant. In the case of non-fiction, this includes verifying resources, cross-referencing, and ensuring all content is true and valid. Then, if the client is happy, it's time to start pounding out the words until a final draft is ready. Once the client is happy and the editing is done, I have a happy client that gets to sell the book as their own.
